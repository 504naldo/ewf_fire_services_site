Smoke Control System Testing

Testing and verification performed in alignment with applicable building code requirements and life safety objectives.

Recently, our team completed a full smoke control system test with multiple specialized trades on site, including an electrician, elevator technician, diesel generator technician, and HVAC technician.

Together, we thoroughly reviewed the entire smoke control system—verifying operation, identifying deficiencies, and documenting observations and recommendations to ensure the system performs as designed during an emergency.

This type of coordinated testing is critical. Smoke control systems are complex, interdependent, and often overlooked until there's a problem. Proper testing helps building owners understand how their life safety systems truly operate, not just how they're supposed to.
