Privacy Policy

How EWF Fire Services collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: May 28, 2026

What We Collect

When you submit a contact or quote request form on this website, we collect the information you provide — which may include your name, company or property name, email address, phone number, and service details.

We also collect standard web server log data (such as IP addresses and browser type) for security and performance purposes. This data is not linked to individual identities.

How We Use Your Information

Your contact information is used solely to respond to your service inquiry and, where applicable, to schedule inspections or services at your property. We do not use your information for commercial email marketing, and we do not sell or share it with third parties except where required to deliver the service you requested.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions and associated correspondence are retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the business purpose for which they were collected, or as required by applicable law.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you. To make such a request, contact us at the information below.
